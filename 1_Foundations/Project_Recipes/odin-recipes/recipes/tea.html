Tea

A warm and inviting brew that soothes the soul, this tea is the perfect blend of robust flavor and refreshing aroma. whether served black, with a splash of milk, or infused with aromatic spices like cardamom and ginger, each sip offers a moment of relaxation. It is the ideal companion for crisp mornings or lazy afternoons, rejuvenating your senses with its delicate balance of strength and warmth.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Tea dust
  • for sweetness sugar

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, bring the water to a boil along with the crushed ginger and cardamom.
  2. Add the tea leaves (or tea powder) and sugar. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes until the water turns dark red.
  3. Pour in the milk and bring the mixture to a boil again.
  4. Once the tea rises to the top of the pan, lower the heat and let it simmer for another minute to thicken.
  5. Turn off the heat, strain the tea into cups using a sieve, and serve hot.
