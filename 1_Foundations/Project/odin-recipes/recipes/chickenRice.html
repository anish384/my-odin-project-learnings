Chicken Rice

This classic comfort dish features succulent pieces of tender chicken served atop a bed of fluffy, aromatic rice. Often cooked in a savory chicken broth infused with garlic, ginger, and subtle spices, every grain absorbs the rich essence of the meat. It is a wholesome, one-pot meal that balances simple textures with deep, satisfying flavors, making it perfect for a hearty lunch or dinner

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Spices like cloves etc
  • For seasoning salt
  • For Garnish Coriander leaves

Ingredients

  1. Wash the Basmati rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a heavy-bottomed pot. Add the whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom).
  3. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and season with salt and pepper. Sear the chicken on high heat for 3-4 minutes until it turns white.
  5. Pour in the water (or chicken stock) and bring it to a boil.
  6. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the boiling water. Stir gently.
  7. Cover the pot with a tight lid, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the water is absorbed and rice is cooked.
  8. Fluff the rice gently with a fork and garnish with coriander leaves before serving.
