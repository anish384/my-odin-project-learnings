Noodles

A vibrant and versatile favorite, these noodles are stir-fried to perfection, offering a delightful chewiness in every bite. Tossed with a colorful medley of crunchy vegetables and your choice of protein, they are coated in a savory, tangy sauce that brings everything together. From the first slurpy strand to the last, this dish delivers a harmonious mix of textures and bold, umami-packed flavors.

Ingredients

  • plain noodles
  • vegetables
  • ginger garlic paste

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot with a pinch of salt and oil. Add noodles and cook until "al dente" (cooked but firm). Drain and rinse with cold water to stop cooking.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over high heat. Add chopped garlic and ginger, saut√©ing quickly for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the onions and vegetables (cabbage, carrots, capsicum). Stir-fry on high heat for 2 minutes (keep them crunchy).
  4. If using chicken or eggs, add the cooked protein now.
  5. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, and chili sauce. Mix well.
  6. Toss in the boiled noodles and season with salt and pepper. Stir-fry for another 2 minutes to coat the noodles evenly.
  7. Garnish with chopped spring onion greens and serve immediately.
